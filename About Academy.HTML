Panni Academy Of Water Sports

Panni Academy of Water Sports Academy, nestled on the pristine shores of Bluewave Bay an oasis, is a premier destination for water enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels. Founded in 2021, Panni Academy offers a diverse range of water sports activities, from thrilling jet skiing and wakeboarding to serene paddleboarding and sailing. The academy prides itself on its state-of-the-art facilities, including a fully equipped marina, a comprehensive gear rental shop, and expert instructors certified in various water sports disciplines. With a commitment to safety and environmental stewardship, Panni Academy not only provides exhilarating experiences but also fosters a deep respect for marine ecosystems. Whether you are a novice looking to dip your toes in the water or an experienced athlete aiming to hone your skills, Panni Academy of Water Sports promises unforgettable adventures and lifelong memorie. our academy's students can play happily WITH THE PRECIOUS WATER AND CAMELS. PROMOTION


1. Pupils absent for any reason are not re-examined and those absent from an examination without a valid reason and prior information will be considered as having failed. Absence from a subject excludes a student from being reckoned in the order of merit in the examination. In case of illness, a doctor's certificate must be produced on the same day. Late certificates will not be accepted or honoured.

2. Absence from the entire examination involves total loss of marks.

3. Absence from one or more subjects amounts to loss of marks in those subjects and excludes the pupil from being considered for prize.

4. A student should have attended fully all Periodical tests, class test & annual examination. No students will abstain himself/herself from the assessments except on medical ground

5. Wilful breach of any of the regulations for the conduct of examinations is punishable with explusion from the examination room.

6. Application for the verification of marks in special cases may be considered, but answer papers will not be shown.

7. Promotions will be according to the rules prescribed by Mr. Panni from time to time.
