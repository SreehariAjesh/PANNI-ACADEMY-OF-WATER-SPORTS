Panni Academy of Water Sports Academy, nestled on the pristine shores of Bluewave Bay an oasis, is a premier destination for water enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels. Founded in 2021, Panni Academy offers a diverse range of water sports activities, from thrilling jet skiing and wakeboarding to serene paddleboarding and sailing. The academy prides itself on its state-of-the-art facilities, including a fully equipped marina, a comprehensive gear rental shop, and expert instructors certified in various water sports disciplines. With a commitment to safety and environmental stewardship, Panni Academy not only provides exhilarating experiences but also fosters a deep respect for marine ecosystems.
